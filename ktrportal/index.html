Solutions

KTR has years of experience with complex, business critical projects, also in the multinational environment, where integration is especially challenging.

Companies with global operations not only need to cope with large distances, but also with time differences, cultural diversity and a range of governmental regulations affecting the workplace. KTR has worked extensively with enterprises in North America, and Asia to understand the complexity of communication between these diverse cultures. Our consultant staff regularly takes part in trainings and is always up-to-date on new technologies such as Cloud Computing, Hybrid Integration Platforms, Elasticsearch or TIBCO ActiveMatrix BusinessWorksTM 6.0. We are committed to training and certifying our consultants in:

  • Service Oriented Architecture (SOA)
  • Business Process Management (BPM)
  • Business Optimization
  • DevOps Lifecycle of Continuous Integration
  • Master Data Management (MDM)
  • Complex Event Processing
  • Enterprise Message Service (EMS)
